Every incident above severity 2 gets a written postmortem within 48 hours of resolution. Without a deadline, postmortems were written weeks later once the details were forgotten. The template asks for a timeline, contributing factors, and action items with owners. Never name an individual as the cause in a postmortem, only the contributing factors. infra/incident-response-runbook

Action items from postmortems are tracked the same way as any other ticket, with an owner and a due date.
